F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What ingredients are used in your hair creams?

Our hair creams are made with a blend of natural ingredients such as shea butter, coconut oil, argan oil, and essential vitamins. We prioritize the use of organic and cruelty-free ingredients to promote healthy hair.

Are your hair creams suitable for all hair types?

Yes, our hair creams are designed to be suitable for all hair types, including curly, straight, dry, and oily hair. We offer a variety of formulas that cater to different hair needs, whether you're looking to hydrate, nourish, or strengthen your hair.

How often should I use the hair cream?

For best results, we recommend using our hair creams 2-3 times a week, depending on your hair type and needs. If you have dry or damaged hair, you can use it more frequently for added moisture and repair.

Are your hair creams safe for color-treated hair?

Yes, our hair creams are safe for color-treated hair. They are formulated with gentle ingredients that help maintain the vibrancy of your color while providing nourishment and moisture to your hair.

Can I use the hair cream on my scalp?

Yes, our hair creams can be applied to your scalp. However, if you have sensitive skin or specific scalp conditions, we recommend doing a patch test before full application. Our formulations are gentle and safe for most scalp types.

Do your hair creams have a fragrance?

Yes, our hair creams have a light, natural fragrance from essential oils like lavender and chamomile. We ensure that the scent is not overpowering and is suitable for all-day use without irritation.
